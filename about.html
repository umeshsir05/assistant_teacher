About LangPedagogy

LangPedagogy is a digital knowledge hub dedicated to the art and science of teaching languages. Built for teacher trainers, graduate students, and practicing educators, the site bridges theory and practice in second/foreign language education.

Our mission: demystify key methodologies, promote inclusive classrooms, and foster reflective teaching. Each chapter includes core concepts, illustrative examples, and reflective prompts.

📌 Why pedagogy of language?

Language teaching has evolved from grammar-translation to communicative, task-based, and plurilingual approaches. Staying updated ensures that learners thrive in multilingual, intercultural environments.

🎯 What we offer

  • 9 comprehensive chapters covering foundations, theories, teaching methods, skills, assessment, TLM, and remedial teaching.
  • Exam-relevant content with references to CG Vyapam TET and other pedagogy exams.
  • Practical classroom strategies, definitions, and comparative tables.
  • Mobile-friendly design with dark mode and accessible navigation.
