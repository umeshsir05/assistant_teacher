📙 Chapter 3

Role of Listening and Speaking

🎧 Listening (श्रवण कौशल)

Listening Skill

Listening skills are skills that contribute to your ability to accurately receive information when communicating with others. These skills are an important part of effective communication in the workplace. Listening method is helpful in permanent learning. While listening activities are activities that are completed by the students when they are listening passage.

For Pronunciation (उच्चारण हेतु)

Listening is a natural process through which children develop skill of pure pronunciation and children become able to overcome the difficulties related to pronunciation.

Growth of Vocabulary / Dictionary (शब्दकोश में वृद्धि)

The process of listening increases the children's dictionary through which its language development is increased.

Differentiation of Sounds (ध्वनियों में अंतर)

Listening is the best method to differentiate in sounds. Children learn to differentiate among different types of sounds, which develops their listening skill as well as reading, writing and speaking skills.

🗣️ Speaking (वाचन कौशल)

Helps in Avoiding Hesitation (हिचकिचाहट को दूर करने में सहायक)

Speaking is a process through which language learning can be overcome by the hesitation in children. During the teaching process in the classroom, some children learn in a very comfortable posture, but when the teacher asks them something, they become silent. A teacher can encourage students to speak a new language by allowing them to speak freely while tolerating their mistake.

Speaking Skills (वाचन कौशल)

Speaking skills are defined as the skills which allow us to communicate effectively. They give us the ability to convey information verbally and in a way that the listener can understand. Crying of a child is sign language.

Making Fluent in Speaking Skill (वाचन कौशल में पारंगत)

Speaking is the means of language development process. Child can develop proficiency and skill in its language fluency. Skill is developed with the development of his language and at the same time its proficiency on the language become stronger. Stuttering the most common fluency disorder.

How Children Use Language as a Tool (बच्चों द्वारा उपकरण के रूप में प्रयोग)

As a communication means (संचार के साधन के रूप में)

Children use a form of language to communicate their thoughts, needs, desires, and express their feelings.

Social Relation (सामाजिक संबंध)

They maintain their relation with the society. Share their culture by interacting with the help of their language. Exchange of ideas between two or more persons is communication.

Repetition of words (शब्दों की पुनरावृत्ति)

Children apply the words and sentence previously listened to at other places to communicate with others.

To generate new ideas (नए विचारों की उत्पत्ति)

Children develop their thinking abilities with the use of language which helps in generating new ideas.

Writing skill (लेखन कौशल)

Writing and speaking correctly gives anyone the appearance of credibility. Grammar helps in making anyone proficient in these areas. Correcting grammar in writing improves the writing skill.

Language complexity (भाषा जटिलता)

Language is a complex system. It has logic in the form of grammar.

Importance of grammar (व्याकरण का महत्व)

Grammar helps in learning correct pronunciation. Knowledge of grammar improves skill of expression. Grammar also helps in acquiring fluency in a particular language. With the knowledge of grammar child develops an effective writing style.

📖 Methods of Teaching Grammar

Inductive Method (आगमनात्मक पद्धति)

Method: Inductive method is a process where learners discover the grammar rules themselves by examining the examples. In an inductive approach it is also possible to use a context for grammar rules. That is to say, learners explore the grammar rules in a text or an audio rather than isolated sentences.

Characteristics:

  • From example to rule
  • From macro to micro
  • Specific to General
  • From known to unknown
  • From the tangible to the intangible

Deductive Method (निगमनात्मक पद्धति)

Method: The principles of this approach are generally used in the classes where the main target is to teach grammar structures. Deductive teaching is a traditional approach in which information about target language and rules are driven at the beginning of the class and continued with examples. Deductive method is a method of teaching used in teaching grammar to the students.

Characteristics:

  • Micro to macro
  • From general to specific
  • From unknown to known
  • From rule to example
Pedagogical Insight: Listening and speaking are foundational skills. Listening enhances pronunciation, vocabulary, and sound discrimination. Speaking overcomes hesitation and builds fluency. Both are tools for communication, social relation, idea generation, and writing. Grammar teaching can be inductive (discovery-based) or deductive (rule-first), each with its own merits.
